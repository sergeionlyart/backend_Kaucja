## Page 6

Fact sheets
Fact sheets provide a systematic overview of the landmark decisions
of the Court of Justice and the General Court on a specific topic of
EU law. They are available in all official EU languages.
On this page, you can access Case-law Fact sheets prepared by the
Research and Documentation Directorate. These fact sheets bring
together a selection of abstracts of the EU Courts’ decisions on a
specific topic.
They are intended as a working tool, organised into easily
accessible sub-topics. They identify the key decisions on a specific
legal issue, particularly where that issue has been the subject of
multiple judgments leading to settled case-law.
